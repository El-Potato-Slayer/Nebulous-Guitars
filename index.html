How it works

Get your guitar in 4 simple steps

1- Search

On the homepage, enter the name of your current location and click on the search button and you will be directed to a page with all the stores within or closest to your area.

2- Locate

Pick a store from the list then you will be provided with all the store details including contact details, address and a map, which is able to give you real-time directions.

3- Choose

Now for the most important step- to choose a guitar that matches your style, personality and, most importantly, your level of drip. If none of the guitars live up to your amount of swag, don't worry. You're able to order custom-made guitars as well!

4- Shred

Once you've acquired your guitar, do whatever you want with it. Shred with it, burn it or perhaps throw it at your annoying neighbor who can't keep their dogs quiet! (Nebulous Guitars is not liable for any injuries done to your neighbors)
